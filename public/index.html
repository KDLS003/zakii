How to claim your item?

Step 1:

Look for your item at our “find it” page and check if the details match your item information.

Step 2:

Prepare your School ID and Visit HAU Campus Services Office located at first floor, Don Juan Bldg. near the Center for Kapampangan Studies

Step 3:

Provide the necessary information needed and claim your valuables.

What is fHAUnd it?

fhaund it is a lost and found web based system created by Holy Angel University’s School of Computing students, In collaboration with HAU Campus Services Office, Our goal is to provide a more convenient method of a lost and found service to our fellow angelites.
